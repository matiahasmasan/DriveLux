DriveLux is a premium car rental service specializing in luxury, sports, and exotic vehicles. Our fleet includes luxury sedans (Mercedes-Benz S-Class, BMW 7 Series), luxury SUVs (Range Rover, Cadillac Escalade), sports cars (Porsche 911, Corvette), and exotic vehicles (Ferrari, Lamborghini). We also offer convertibles and electric luxury vehicles. Our inventory varies by location, so please check our Fleet page for current availability.

Our headquarters is in Beverly Hills, California with branches in Miami (Downtown, Airport Terminal, and South Beach). Our standard business hours vary by location, generally 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM on weekdays with modified weekend hours. Our Airport Terminal location is open daily from 6:00 AM to 12:00 AM. We also offer after-hours pickup and drop-off by appointment. Visit our Locations page for specific addresses and hours for each branch.

You can make reservations online through our website, by calling (800) DRIVE-LUX, through our mobile app, or by visiting any location in person. You'll need to provide your name, contact information, desired pickup/return dates and locations, vehicle preference, valid driver's license information, and payment method for the deposit. We recommend booking in advance, especially for exotic and high-demand vehicles.

Age requirements vary by vehicle category: Luxury Sedans & SUVs (minimum age 21), Sports Cars (minimum age 25), Exotic Cars (minimum age 30, with ages 25-29 eligible with additional fee and insurance), and Ultra-Exotic Cars (minimum age 30 with proof of exotic car ownership or rental experience). All drivers must have a valid driver's license held for at least 2 years.

We accept major credit cards, debit cards with credit logos, digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay), and approved corporate accounts. At reservation, we charge a 25% deposit, with the remaining 75% charged 7 days before pickup. For last-minute bookings, the full amount is charged at reservation. A security deposit ($1,000-$25,000 depending on vehicle type) is held at pickup and released upon satisfactory return of the vehicle.

Yes, we offer several discount programs including extended rental discounts (10-15% off for 7+ days), corporate rates, repeat customer loyalty program benefits, seasonal promotions, partner hotel guest discounts (5-10% with proof of stay), and military/first responder discounts (10% with valid ID). Visit our Special Offers page for current promotions and details.

Our mileage allowances depend on the vehicle category: Luxury Sedans & SUVs (150 miles per day), Sports Cars (100 miles per day), Exotic Cars (75 miles per day), and Ultra-Exotic Cars (50 miles per day). Additional mileage can be purchased in advance at discounted rates. Unlimited mileage packages are available for some vehicle categories for extended rentals.

Yes, we offer airport pickup and drop-off services at major airports in our service areas. This service includes having a DriveLux representative meet you at your terminal with your vehicle. We also have a dedicated location at Miami International Airport. Additionally, we offer vehicle delivery and collection services to hotels, residences, and other locations within our service areas for an additional fee.

We offer several insurance coverage options: Standard Coverage (included with all rentals, provides basic liability and damage waiver with $2,000-$10,000 deductible), Premium Coverage ($39-$99 per day, reduces deductible to $1,000-$2,500), and Full Coverage ($79-$199 per day, reduces deductible to $0-$500). We also offer supplemental liability and personal effects coverage. For exotic vehicles, we may require our coverage regardless of your personal insurance.

In case of damage, report it immediately to our customer service team and complete an incident report with photos. Your security deposit will be held pending damage assessment. With our premium insurance coverage, your liability is limited to the deductible. Without insurance coverage, you're responsible for all damages up to the full value of the vehicle. For major accidents, contact emergency services first, then notify DriveLux immediately.
