Our Hiring Process

1

Application Review

Submit your resume and cover letter. Our HR team will review your application within 5 business days.

2

Initial Interview

A 30-minute phone or video interview with a hiring manager to discuss your background and experience.

3

Skills Assessment

Depending on the role, you may be asked to complete a skills assessment or practical demonstration.

4

Team Interview

Meet with the team you'll be working with to ensure a good cultural fit and answer any questions.

5

Offer & Onboarding

Receive your offer and join the DriveLux family with a comprehensive onboarding program.
